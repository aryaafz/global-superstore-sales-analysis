# 📊 Global Superstore Sales Analysis & Interactive Power BI Dashboard


## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the **Global Superstore** dataset to uncover sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and regional trends.

The project follows a complete data analytics workflow, starting from **data cleaning**, **feature engineering**, **exploratory data analysis (EDA)** using Python, and ending with an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that provides actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Problems

This project aims to answer the following business questions:

1. How have sales and profit changed over time?
2. Which product categories and sub-categories generate the highest sales and profit?
3. Which products are underperforming despite high sales?
4. How does discount impact profitability?
5. Which markets, regions, and countries contribute the most to revenue?
6. Who are the company's top customers?
7. How is sales distributed across different customer segments?

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
- Identify top-performing and underperforming products.
- Evaluate the impact of discount on profitability.
- Analyze regional sales performance.
- Identify valuable customers.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

### 📈 Sales Performance

- Sales increased consistently from 2011 to 2014.
- Profit followed the same upward trend, indicating healthy business growth.

### 🖥 Product Performance

- Technology generated the highest total profit.
- Furniture produced the lowest profit among all categories.
- Phones generated the highest sales.
- Copiers delivered the highest profit.
- Tables generated negative profit despite relatively high sales.

### 👥 Customer Analysis

- Consumer segment contributed the largest portion of sales.
- A small number of customers generated a significant portion of total revenue.

### 🌍 Regional Analysis

- APAC contributed the highest sales among all markets.
- Central region generated the highest regional sales.

### 💸 Discount Analysis

- Profit decreases as discount increases.
- Discounts above 40% significantly reduce profitability.
- Very high discounts (>60%) often lead to financial losses.

---

# 📈 Business Recommendations

### Product Strategy

- Continue investing in Technology products.
- Review pricing strategy for Tables due to continuous losses.
- Increase marketing for high-margin products such as Copiers and Phones.

### Discount Strategy

- Limit excessive discounts above 40%.
- Introduce targeted promotions instead of blanket discounts.
- Monitor products frequently sold with high discounts.

### Regional Strategy

- Continue expanding operations in APAC.
- Improve sales strategies in lower-performing markets.

### Customer Strategy

- Strengthen loyalty programs for top customers.
- Increase engagement with Consumer segment while exploring growth opportunities in Corporate and Home Office segments.
